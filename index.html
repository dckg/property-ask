About

Hi, I'm Chee How.

I'm a realtor in Singapore, focused on Districts 5, 10, 11, 21 — and beyond. I can help clients across HDB resale, private condominiums, landed homes, and new launches.

Whether you're upgrading from a flat, right-sizing after the kids move out, or planning the next move in your asset progression, I keep things clear, end-to-end, and aligned with your actual goals — including the financial planning that sits behind each property decision.

How I work

Four things you can expect.

  • Clear advice.

    Plain English, plain numbers. No jargon, no guessing. You'll know what each step costs and what each choice means.

  • End-to-end support.

    From valuation and pricing through viewings, negotiations, paperwork, and completion — I'm there for the whole thing.

  • Aligned with your goals.

    Every recommendation is grounded in data, timing, and your financial priorities — never just the next deal.

  • Long-term strategy.

    Asset progression and the financial planning behind it — mapping the path from HDB to private, restructuring a portfolio, financing the next decade. Strategy, not just the next sale.
